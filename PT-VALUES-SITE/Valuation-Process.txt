DEMAND: How often we find it in trades. Added if the collectible has a value.
From left to right, it goes from the worst demand to best:

None - Awful - Low - Average - High - Great


TREND: Its recent performance (in sales and trades). It is added if the collectible has a value.
From left to right, it goes from the worst performance to best:

None - Lowering - Unstable - Stable - Rising

There is also a "Projected" tag.
